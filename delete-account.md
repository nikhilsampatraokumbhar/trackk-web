# Delete your Trackk account

We respect your right to walk away. You can permanently delete your
Trackk account and all of the personal data associated with it.

## Option 1 — In-app (recommended, fastest)

1. Open Trackk.
2. Go to **Profile** (bottom-right tab).
3. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Delete Account**.
4. Confirm. Your account, transactions, groups, goals, and any data
   stored in our cloud are scheduled for immediate deletion.

You will be signed out. The app will continue to work in local-only
mode if you re-install it — none of your previous data will be
recovered.

## Option 2 — By email

If you cannot access the app (lost phone, locked out, etc.), email us
from the address you used to sign up to:

> **support@trackk2save.com**
> Subject: **Account deletion request**

Please include the email address or phone number you signed up with.
We may ask you to verify ownership before processing the request to
prevent malicious deletions.

## What gets deleted

When your account is deleted:

- Your **profile, transactions, goals, budgets, custom categories, and
  notes** are permanently removed from our active systems.
- **Your private cloud data** in Cloud Firestore (if cloud sync was
  enabled) is deleted immediately.
- **Authentication records** are deleted from Firebase Authentication.
- **Backups** are purged within 30 days from the deletion date as part
  of our rolling-backup rotation.

## What may be retained

We may retain a small amount of data, only where law requires us to,
for the periods specified in our
[Privacy Policy](/privacy/#7-how-long-we-keep-your-data):

- Records required for **tax, accounting, or dispute-resolution
  obligations** (typically for the period required by Indian law).
- **Aggregated, de-identified analytics** that cannot be traced back to
  you.
- **Group expenses you participated in** continue to exist on the group
  document while the group itself still exists (so the other members'
  ledgers don't break), but your personal identifiers are removed
  from your splits.

## How long it takes

Deletion is processed **within 30 days** of your request, in line with
the timelines in the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023
(India) and the GDPR.

## Questions

Write to **support@trackk2save.com** any time.
